Mission

LoveBucks (LB) is a complementary currency system strengthening local organizations, empowering individuals and building community in the neighborhoods of North Philadelphia.

Goals

  • Assist neighborhood organizations in meeting their needs
  • Empower individuals who are un/underemployed by providing opportunities for work
  • Leverage the knowledge, skills and passions of local residents
  • Help locally owned businesses
  • Keep money circulating in the local economy
  • Strengthen local supply chains
  • Build community and trust in our neighborhoods

How it Works

Getting LoveBucks

You can get LoveBucks by either converting US Dollars (USD) to LoveBucks(LB), or by working on a project at one of the neighborhood organizations and receiving pay in LoveBucks. You can also get LoveBucks by accepting them as change at a participating business, or by listing your services in the LoveBucks Marketplace.

Converting US Dollars to LoveBucks

This is the easiest way to start using LoveBucks. Just stop by one of our exchange locations and convert your USDs to LBs! You will receive 10$LB for $10.

Working for LoveBucks

If you are looking to help a local organization with one of their projects, you can get paid in LBs. This is a great way to meet your neighbors, improve your community and get rewarded for your work! This is also a great way to start earning LBs if you donâ€™t have a lot of USDs to spare.

Using LoveBucks

Once you have some LoveBucks, you can spend them at participating businesses or employ residents offering their skills and talents in the LoveBucks Marketplace.
